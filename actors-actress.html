5 Most expensive actors/actresses in 2021

Subject List:

  • Introduction
  • High paid actors/actress in 2021
  • List of the actors/actresses
  • Epilogue

2020 has been very challenging for actors/actresses across the world as films failed to release at the box office due to the coronavirus pandemic.
However, there has been a sharp recovery in 2021 and Hollywood's top-tier actors have been earning handsomely with the release of their blockbuster films. Although a number of high-budget films failed to release and were even postponed indefinitely, there have been a number of actors who have earned millions of dollars.

With this article, we are here to share with you the list of 5 highest paid actors/actresses in the world in 2021.

Every year, Forbes magazine publishes a list of the most expensive actors/actresses in the world based on their salary from June 1 of last year to June 1 of this year.
Here is the best 5 paid actors and actresses:

British actor Daniel Craig is now the world's highest-paid actor thanks to his lucrative contract with the Netflix platform, signed in 2021.
His 2019 escapist mystery comedy Knives Out has two sequels on Netflix.
Netflix, which has bought the rights to broadcast the sequels, will pay him $100 million, which is why Daniel Craig takes first place in this year's list of the most expensive foreign male actors.

Scarlett Johansson is the most expensive actress in Hollywood with an income of 56 million dollars.
She has been the first person in the list of "Most expensive actresses" since 2018.
One of her most important and famous roles is the role of Black Widow, which she has played in eight different movies such as Avengers: Endgame.

Currently, Dwayne Douglas Johnson is on the list of the most expensive male actors in the world for the third year in a row with an income of 50 million dollars.
Known as The Rock, he was one of the top wrestlers in the world for eight years before turning to acting.
His films have grossed more than 10.5 billion dollars worldwide, making him one of the most expensive male actors in Hollywood.

Sofia Vergara an American-Colombian actress, was born on July 10, 1972.
She was the highest paid actress on TV in 2012, 2013 and 2016.
Her first major English-language film was Chasing Papi in 2003.
Since 2009, she has appeared on the ABC sitcom Modern Family.
Her success in television made her prolific in cinema as well. With an income of more than 43 million dollars,
Vergara is ranked fourth in the list of the most expensive actors/actresses in Hollywood in 2021.

After a year's absence, Will Smith's name has once again entered the list of the world's most expensive actors/actresses.
It is interesting to know that he is the only actor whose 11 titles of his films were consistently successful and grossed more than 150 million dollars worldwide.
Bad Boys for Life (2020) earned him $40 million, making him one of the most expensive male actors in Hollywood today.
