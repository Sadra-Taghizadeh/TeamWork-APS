Cinema Paradiso

Cinema Paradiso (Italian: Nuovo Cinema Paradiso, literally "New Paradise Cinema") is a 1988 coming-of-age drama film written and directed by Giuseppe Tornatore. Set in a small Sicilian town, the film centers on the friendship between a young boy and an aging projectionist who works at the titular movie theatre.
